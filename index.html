Digital Marketing Course Eligibility

In order to pursue any digital marketing certificate or course, students need to complete their graduation from a relevant stream from a recognized board. Some of the digital marketing certificates or programs require students to have 2 or more years of work experience in the related fields.

Course Digital Marketing Course Qualification
Certificate in Digital Marketing Students need to complete class 12 or graduation in most cases. Some digital marketing certificate courses require work experience in the related field.
Diploma in Digital Marketing Students need to complete graduation in a relevant stream from a recognized board.
BBA Digital Marketing Students need to pass class 10 and class 12 exams with 50% aggregate score.
MSc or MBA Digital Marketing Students need to complete graduation in a relevant stream and score more than 50% marks.
